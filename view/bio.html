Niaz-Murshed Bio
Born May 13, 1966. Dhaka, Bangladesh
Early Life Murshed passed his SSC exam in 1983 from St. Joseph High School and HSC exam in 1985 from Dhaka College
Career He played for Bangladesh in the Chess Olympiads of 1984, 1990, 1994, 1996, 2002, 2004, 2012 and 2014
Education University of Pennsylvania
Style to play Murshed plays primarily positional chess. He builds up small advantages, ultimately leading to a victory.
Title GrandMaster ( 1987)
